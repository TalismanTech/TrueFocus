meet the founder

Unlock Your True Focus

Arguably one of, if not the best skills trainer in the country, Coach Jolo Tamayo is licensed and mentored by the world renowned skills trainer, Drew Hanlen, Founder of Pure Sweat, who has players like Joel Embiid and Jayson Tatum under them.

Now, Coach Jolo trains international Filipino Players like Kiefer Ravena, Thirdy Ravena, Bobby Ray Parks Jr, and Kobe Paras. He also trains local Filipino Collegiate and Professional Players of all positions, which goes to show the versatility of his coaching.

Quick answers to your top questions

Everything you need to know, fast.

What skills can I work on?

We help you get better at shooting, dribbling, defense, and understanding the game. All skill levels are welcome.

Who are the coaches?

Our coaches are friendly, experienced, and have worked with all ages—even pros. Each brings their own style to help you improve.

Can I try a session first?

Absolutely! You can book a single session before availing of our packages. Just contact us to get started.

Are group and private lessons available?

Yes, you can join group classes or book one-on-one sessions. Pick what works best for you and your schedule.
